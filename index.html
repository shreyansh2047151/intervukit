Platform Overview


ResuMatch


ResuMatch is a web-based application designed to analyze resumes against a given job description using AI. It categorizes resumes into Best, Better, and Good fits based on their relevance. The analysis is powered by the Gemini AI API.

Interview Kit Generator


The Interview Kit Generator is a web-based tool that helps users generate a customized interview kit using AI. By analyzing the resume and job description, the platform creates tailored interview questions, quick Q&A, company insights, and a candidate analysis report in PDF format.
